The Need for a Dynamic Grant System

The origins of my thesis topic.

This map represents university students from Qatar that are on grants to study abroad. As a country, Qatar is planning on being an economically sustainable society that is now exports its expertise and knowledge instead of oil and gas.

To reach our goals, many initiatives have taken place to enable a future generations that is equipped to tackle issues on a global scale. We need to grow self sufficient when it comes to importing knowledge, an unsustainable practice that happens just because its affordable. For now.

Science, Medicine, Education, and Research are fields with the lowest amount of Qataris working in them. So if the solutions were deployed, the type of fields students are studying should reflect the need.

Here is a chart that shows the number of new grants approved in 2015:

As you can see, the number of grants utilized for important fields such as Science, Medicine and Education are extremely low compared to others. In fact, 23% of students abroad on grants are studying business.
